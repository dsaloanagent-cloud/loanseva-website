Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Credit Card (Salaried)?

Simple eligibility for salaried employees across private sector, government, and PSU organisations in AP & Telangana.

💼 Basic Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 60 years for primary cardholder. Add-on card for family members from 18 years.

  • 💰

    Minimum Salary

    ₹15,000/month net salary for entry-level cards. ₹50,000+ for premium cards like HDFC Regalia, Axis Magnus.

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700+ preferred. Some banks approve 650+ for entry-level cards with good salary and stable employment.

  • 💼

    Employment Type

    Private sector, government employee, PSU employee, MNC employee — all eligible. Minimum 6 months with current employer.

  • 📋

    ITR Filed

    Not mandatory for basic cards. Required for premium cards (HDFC Infinia, Axis Reserve) with high credit limits.

💡 Government employees get preferential treatment at SBI, PNB, Canara Bank — even with lower salaries. Agent will check which bank's policy best fits your employer category.

📈 Salary Vs Card Category

  • 💳

    ₹15K – ₹25K / Month

    Entry cards — SBI SimplySAVE, HDFC MoneyBack+, ICICI Coral. Good cashback, no annual fee options.

  • 💳

    ₹25K – ₹50K / Month

    Mid-tier cards — SBI SimplyCLICK, Axis Neo, HDFC Millennia, ICICI Amazon Pay. Better rewards & lounge access.

  • 💳

    ₹50K – ₹1L / Month

    Premium cards — HDFC Regalia, Axis Vistara, SBI Elite, Amex MRCC. Multiple lounge visits, higher rewards.

  • 💳

    Above ₹1L / Month

    Super premium — HDFC Infinia, Axis Magnus, Amex Platinum. Unlimited lounge, concierge, 5X rewards on all spends.

💡 No CIBIL or low CIBIL? Apply for a secured credit card (against FD) — builds credit history in 6–12 months. Agent will help you choose the right one.

Employee Benefits

Why salaried employees love credit cards

A credit card used wisely is a powerful financial tool — not just a payment method. Here's why every salaried person should have one.

💰 Earn While You Spend

Every grocery purchase, dinner, fuel fill, flight booking earns you cashback or reward points. An average salaried person can earn ₹5,000–₹15,000/year in rewards alone.

✈️ Free Airport Lounge Access

Premium cards give 4–12 complimentary domestic lounge visits and 4 international visits per year — each worth ₹500–₹2,500. Annual benefit alone covers card fee.

⏳ 50 Days Interest-Free Credit

Buy now, pay 50 days later — zero interest. This improves your monthly cash flow significantly, especially between pay cycles.

📈 Builds Credit Score

Using and paying credit card on time is the fastest way to improve CIBIL score — making future home loans and car loans cheaper to access.

🛡️ Zero Fraud Liability

Credit cards offer better fraud protection than debit cards. Unauthorized transactions are reversed — unlike debit cards where money is instantly debited.

🎊 Exclusive Offers & Discounts

10–30% discounts on Swiggy, Zomato, Amazon, Myntra, MakeMyTrip, BookMyShow — exclusive deals only for credit card holders.

🎁

How Reward Points Work

Points earned on every spend → redeemed for flight tickets, Amazon vouchers, hotel stays, or cashback. 1 point = ₹0.25–₹0.50 typical value. Premium cards offer 1 point = ₹1 on flights.

✈️

Lounge Access Value

Domestic lounge visit = ₹500–₹800 value. International = ₹1,500–₹2,500. A card with 8 domestic visits = ₹4,000–₹6,400 annual value alone — often more than the card's annual fee.

🔄

Annual Fee Waiver

Most cards waive annual fee if you spend above a threshold — typically ₹1L–₹2L/year. Agent selects cards where waiver condition matches your natural spending pattern.

💳

Credit Limit Formula

Banks typically set credit limit at 2x–4x your monthly salary. With good CIBIL and multiple salary slips, you can get 5x limit. Existing cardholders can request limit enhancement annually.

⚠️

Smart Usage Tips

Keep utilisation below 30% of limit. Pay full outstanding monthly — avoid minimum payment trap (40% interest!). Set auto-pay for full amount. Never miss due date.
