Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for Loan Against Property?

Simple eligibility norms for salaried, self-employed, and business owners who own clear-titled property.

👔 Salaried Applicants

  • 🎂

    Age

    23 to 65 years at loan maturity

  • 💼

    Work Experience

    Minimum 2 years total, 1 year in current organisation

  • 💰

    Minimum Income

    ₹25,000/month net salary preferred

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700 or above preferred for best rates

💡 Co-applicants (spouse / family member) can be added to increase loan eligibility amount. Agent will guide on the best combination.

🏢 Self-Employed / Business Owners

  • 🎂

    Age

    25 to 70 years at the time of application

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 3 years of business / professional practice

  • 💰

    Minimum Turnover / Income

    ITR-based income assessment, ₹2 Lakhs+ annual income preferred

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    680 or above preferred (lower scores also considered case-by-case)

💡 NRI Property Owners are also eligible with additional documentation. Agent will confirm exact requirements for your case.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Clear property documents speed up sanction significantly. We collect digitally or from your home/office.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Recent utility bill for address proof

🏠 Property Documents

  • Sale Deed / Title Deed (original)
  • Property tax receipts (latest)
  • Approved building plan / occupancy certificate
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC) — 13/30 years

💰 Income Documents

  • Last 3 months salary slips (salaried)
  • Last 6 months bank statement
  • Last 2–3 years ITR with financials (self-employed)
  • GST returns / business proof (if applicable)

📸 Photographs & Others

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Signed application form
  • Existing loan statements (if any)
  • Co-applicant documents (if applicable)

📱 Initial documents can be shared digitally via WhatsApp — agent visits for property document verification.

Loan Amount, Tenure & Rates

What can you expect on your LAP?

Indicative figures based on property type and applicant profile — agent gives you exact figures after eligibility check.

🏠 Residential Property

  • Loan up to 65–70% of market value
  • Rate: 8.75% – 11% p.a.
  • Tenure: up to 20 years
  • Amount: ₹5L – ₹3 Crores

🏢 Commercial Property

  • Loan up to 55–65% of market value
  • Rate: 9.5% – 12% p.a.
  • Tenure: up to 15 years
  • Amount: ₹10L – ₹5 Crores

🏭 Industrial / Plot

  • Loan up to 50–60% of market value
  • Rate: 10% – 13% p.a.
  • Tenure: up to 10–12 years
  • Amount: ₹10L – ₹3 Crores

📊 Processing & Charges

  • Processing fee: 0.5% – 1.5% of loan
  • Legal & valuation charges apply
  • Pre-payment: usually nil for floating rate
  • Stamp duty as per state mortgage rules
