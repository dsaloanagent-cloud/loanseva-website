Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for Fire & Burglary Insurance?

Business owners, shopkeepers, and property owners of any size can apply, with valuation norms varying by sum insured.

🏪 Property Owner / Tenant

  • 📜

    Ownership / Lease Proof

    Property title deed or valid rental/lease agreement required

  • 🏢

    Active Business / Occupancy

    Premises should be in active commercial or industrial use

  • 📋

    Stock & Asset Declaration

    Accurate declaration of stock value, machinery & furniture for correct sum insured

  • 📊

    No Active Claim Rejection

    No prior policy cancellation for fraud or misrepresentation

💡 Both owned and rented premises are eligible — tenants can insure their stock, machinery & fittings even if they don't own the building.

🏭 Business Types Covered

  • 🛍️

    Retail Shops & Showrooms

    General stores, electronics, garments, and other retail outlets

  • 🏭

    Manufacturing Units

    Small & medium factories with machinery and raw material stock

  • 📦

    Warehouses & Godowns

    Storage facilities holding goods, raw material or finished products

  • 🏢

    Offices & Commercial Spaces

    Office furniture, fixtures, computers and equipment covered

💡 Large industrial units with sum insured above ₹1 Crore typically need a property survey before policy issuance — agent coordinates this for you.

Coverage & Property Protection

What kind of property and risks are covered?

Choose the combination of building, stock & machinery cover that fits your business — sum insured is based on declared asset value.

🏢

Building / Structure

Covers the physical structure of shop, factory, warehouse or office against fire and allied perils.

📦

Stock & Inventory

Raw material, finished goods, and trading stock covered against fire, theft and burglary.

⚙️

Plant & Machinery

Manufacturing equipment, machinery and tools covered against fire damage and burglary loss.

🪑

Furniture & Fixtures

Office furniture, fittings, electrical installations and interior fixtures covered under the policy.

Natural Calamity Add-On

Optional cover for flood, storm, earthquake (AOG/EQ) and other natural calamities.

Burglary & Theft Add-On

Protection against forced entry theft and burglary of stock, cash and movable assets.

Premium, Coverage & Claim Process

What can you expect on your policy?

Indicative figures based on property type and declared value — agent gives you exact quotes after comparing insurers.

🏢 Building Cover Component

  • Sum insured based on reinstatement value
  • Premium: based on construction type & location
  • Tenure: 1 / 2 / 3 year options available
  • Amount: ₹5 Lakhs – ₹5 Crore+

📦 Stock & Machinery Component

  • Sum insured based on declared stock value
  • Premium: 0.1%–0.5% of sum insured typically
  • Periodic stock value updation recommended
  • Amount: assessed separately by insurer

➕ Add-On / Rider Component

  • Flood, earthquake, riot, terrorism riders optional
  • Premium: small additional cost per rider
  • Coverage: as per rider terms
  • Amount: 5%–15% of base premium typically

📋 Claim Process

  • Immediate intimation to insurer & agent post-loss
  • Surveyor visits premises to assess loss
  • Documentation: FIR (for theft), photos, stock records
  • Settlement after survey report approval
