Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Farm Equipment Loan?

Individual farmers, joint borrowers, and farming groups across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana are eligible.

👨‍🌾 Farmer Eligibility

  • 🇮🇳

    Nationality

    Indian citizen engaged in agricultural or allied activities

  • 🎂

    Age

    18 to 65 years at time of application. Maximum 70 years at loan end.

  • 🌾

    Land Holding

    Minimum 1 acre own / leased agricultural land. Pattadar passbook mandatory.

  • 📜

    Farmer Type

    Individual farmer, joint farmers, SHG, FPC (Farmer Producer Company), co-operative society

  • 💳

    Credit History

    No willful default on existing loans. Existing KCC holders get priority processing.

💡 Small & Marginal Farmers? NABARD-linked banks and SBI Kisan schemes have special provisions for farmers with less than 2 acres — higher subsidy and lower margin money requirement. Agent confirms eligibility.

📋 Additional Eligibility Conditions

  • 💼

    Purpose

    Equipment must be used for agricultural / allied activities only. Commercial hiring also permitted for custom hiring centres.

  • 🏷️

    Equipment Brand

    Approved brands / dealers only. Bank-empanelled dealers preferred for faster sanction.

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    650+ preferred. Lower scores considered for farmers with strong land records and existing bank relationship.

  • 🏗️

    Additional Collateral

    Land mortgage or existing FD may be required for loans above ₹10 Lakhs depending on bank policy.

💡 Custom Hiring Centre (CHC)? Setting up a CHC to rent equipment to other farmers? SMAM scheme provides 40% capital subsidy — up to ₹25 Lakhs subsidy on ₹1Cr project. Agent handles the entire subsidy application process.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Both farmer and land/equipment documents are needed. Agent helps compile and verify everything — even village visits for document collection.

👨‍🌾 Farmer Identity Documents

  • Aadhaar Card + PAN Card
  • Voter ID / Passport (alternate ID)
  • Caste certificate (for SC/ST/OBC schemes)
  • 2 passport size photographs
  • Farmer registration (if applicable)

🌾 Land & Agricultural Documents

  • Pattadar Passbook / RoR (Record of Rights)
  • Adangal / Pahani (land records)
  • Land possession certificate
  • Lease agreement (if leased land)
  • Last 2 years crop details / produce income

🚜 Equipment & Dealer Documents

  • Equipment quotation from dealer
  • Dealer invoice / pro-forma invoice
  • Equipment make, model, and specification sheet
  • Dealer empanelment letter (bank-approved)
  • Insurance quote for equipment (if required)

🏦 Financial & Bank Documents

  • Last 6 months bank statements (savings/CC)
  • Existing loan details (if any)
  • KCC (Kisan Credit Card) details if applicable
  • Last 2 years ITR / income proof
  • Bank account cancelled cheque

📱 All documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp. Agent will visit your village if needed for physical document help.

Government Schemes & Subsidies

Govt. schemes that reduce your loan burden

Multiple central and state government schemes provide direct capital subsidy on farm equipment — agent handles all applications for you.

🏛️ SMAM — Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanisation

Central Government scheme providing 40–50% capital subsidy to individual farmers for purchase of tractors, power tillers, harvesters, and implements. Small/marginal farmers get maximum benefit.

40–50% Subsidy | All Equipment

🚜 RKVY — Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana

State-level mechanisation fund under RKVY provides additional subsidy for farm machinery not covered under SMAM. AP and Telangana both have active RKVY components for equipment purchase.

State Subsidy | AP & TG Active

☀️ PM-KUSUM — Solar Pump Scheme

60% subsidy on solar irrigation pumps for farmers — 30% central + 30% state government contribution. Farmer pays only 40% (can be financed by bank loan). Reduces electricity cost permanently.

60% Subsidy | Solar Pumps

🏭 CHC — Custom Hiring Centre Scheme

Individual farmers / FPCs setting up Custom Hiring Centres get 40% capital subsidy up to ₹10 Lakhs on ₹25 Lakh project. High-Tech Hubs eligible for up to ₹40 Lakhs subsidy.

40% Subsidy | Up to ₹40L

💧 PMKSY — Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Subsidy on drip and sprinkler irrigation systems — 55% for small/marginal farmers, 45% for others. Applies to equipment cost, installation, and pipeline. One of the most impactful schemes.

55% Subsidy | Drip & Sprinkler

✈️ Agricultural Drone Scheme

Government of India and state governments provide 50–100% subsidy on agricultural drones for registered FPOs, cooperatives, and women SHGs. Individual farmers also eligible for 40% subsidy.

40–100% Subsidy | Drones

💡 Subsidy + Loan Combination: Best strategy — apply for govt. subsidy first, get sanction, then take bank loan only for remaining amount. Effective interest outgo drops drastically. Agent coordinates both processes simultaneously for faster access to equipment.
