Senior Citizen Health Plan

Dedicated health insurance built for life after 60

As parents grow older, routine check-ups, hospitalisation, and medicine costs increase significantly. A Senior Citizen Health Plan is a specialised medical insurance policy designed to support people aged 60 and above, where regular health plans often fall short on coverage or charge very high premiums.

Unlike standard health insurance, a senior citizen plan is underwritten keeping age-related risks in mind — higher hospitalisation chances, longer recovery periods, and a greater likelihood of pre-existing conditions like diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, or heart-related issues. DSA Loan Agent helps families across Andhra Pradesh & Telangana compare plans from leading insurers like Star Health, Care Health, Niva Bupa, and National Insurance — so your parents get genuine protection, not just a policy document.

Whether your parents are completely healthy or already managing a chronic condition, there is a plan suited to their profile. Our role is simple: understand their medical history, compare the right insurers, and help you avoid claim rejections later due to wrong plan selection.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Senior Citizen Health Plan?

Basic criteria to get your parents covered. Healthy or managing a condition — we have a matching plan for every profile.

👵 Healthy Senior Citizens

  • 🎂

    Age

    60 to 75 years at the time of policy purchase (entry age)

  • 🆔

    Residency

    Indian resident with valid ID and address proof from AP or Telangana

  • 🩺

    Medical Checkup

    Some insurers require a basic checkup; many offer no-checkup plans up to a certain sum insured

  • 📊

    Declaration

    Honest disclosure of medical history at the time of proposal — this protects future claims

💡 Generally healthy? You can access lower premiums and faster policy issuance, often without any medical test, depending on the chosen sum insured.

💊 Senior Citizens with Pre-Existing Conditions

  • 🎂

    Age

    60 to 75 years; some insurers also accept proposals up to 80 years with conditions

  • 📋

    Medical Disclosure

    Full disclosure of diabetes, BP, thyroid, heart, or kidney-related conditions is mandatory

  • 🧪

    Medical Tests

    Pre-policy medical screening usually required to assess risk and finalise premium

  • ⏳

    Waiting Period

    Pre-existing diseases are covered after a waiting period, typically 1 to 4 years

💡 Already managing diabetes or BP? Several insurers offer specialised plans with reduced waiting periods for common lifestyle conditions. Agent will identify the right fit.

Coverage & Health Benefits

What does a Senior Citizen Health Plan cover?

Comprehensive medical protection designed around the real healthcare needs of people above 60.

🏥

In-Patient Hospitalisation

Covers room rent, ICU charges, doctor fees, and nursing costs for hospital stays of 24 hours or more.

💉

Pre & Post Hospitalisation

Medical expenses incurred before admission and after discharge — typically 30 to 60 days on either side.

⚕️

Daycare Procedures

Covers modern treatments like cataract surgery and dialysis that don't need a full day of hospitalisation.

💊

Pre-Existing Disease Cover

Diabetes, hypertension, thyroid, and other chronic conditions are covered after the applicable waiting period.

🚑

Ambulance Charges

Reimbursement for emergency ambulance transport to the nearest network or non-network hospital.

🏠

Domiciliary Treatment

Covers home-based treatment when hospitalisation isn't possible due to the patient's condition or bed unavailability.

🔄

No Claim Bonus

Sum insured increases every claim-free year on several plans, boosting cover without raising the premium.

🩹

Critical Illness Add-On

Optional cover for cancer, stroke, and major organ-related illnesses with a lump-sum payout on diagnosis.
