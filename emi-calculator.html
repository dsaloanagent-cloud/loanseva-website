EMI Formula

Standard EMI Calculation Formula Explained

Banks and financial institutions same formula use chestāyi — idi RBI-approved standard method. Simple gā explain chestāmu.

📐 Standard EMI Formula (RBI Standard)

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)n ÷ [(1 + r)n − 1]
P = Principal loan amount (borrowed amount in ₹)
r = Monthly interest rate = Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100
n = Loan tenure in months (years × 12)
EMI = Monthly instalment amount (₹)
📘 Example: ₹10,00,000 loan at 10% p.a. for 5 years (60 months).
Monthly rate r = 10 ÷ 12 ÷ 100 = 0.00833
EMI = 10,00,000 × 0.00833 × (1.00833)^60 ÷ [(1.00833)^60 − 1]
= ₹21,247 per month — Total Interest = ₹2,74,820 — Total Payment = ₹12,74,820
