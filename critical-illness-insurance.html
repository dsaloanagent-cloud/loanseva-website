Eligibility Criteria

Who can buy a Critical Illness Insurance plan?

Any Indian resident between 5 and 65 years of age can purchase critical illness insurance, subject to medical underwriting.

👤 Individual Eligibility

  • 🇮🇳

    Nationality

    Indian resident. NRIs also eligible with some insurers — separate plans available.

  • 🎂

    Entry Age

    Typically 5 to 65 years. Some insurers allow up to 75 years with medical underwriting.

  • 💪

    Health Status

    Must not have been diagnosed with the covered illnesses prior to policy purchase. Pre-existing CI conditions excluded.

  • 📋

    Medical Tests

    Medical examination may be required above certain sum insured or age — insurer-specific. Agent arranges home sample collection.

  • 🚬

    Lifestyle Disclosure

    Smoking, tobacco, alcohol habits must be disclosed honestly. Non-smokers get lower premium rates.

💡 Family History of Cancer / Heart Disease? Some insurers load premium for family history. Agent identifies insurers who do not penalise family medical history — saving you 15–25% on premium.

📋 Plan-Level Conditions

  • ⌛

    Waiting Period

    90 days initial waiting period from policy start date — no claims during this window. Accident-related CI may be exempt.

  • ⏳

    Survival Period

    Policyholder must survive 30 days post-diagnosis. Some plans now offer 0-day survival for specific conditions.

  • 🔁

    Pre-existing Diseases

    Illnesses diagnosed before policy purchase are excluded. Honesty in declaration is critical — undisclosed conditions lead to claim rejection.

  • 📊

    Sum Insured Selection

    Recommended: minimum 5x annual income or ₹25 Lakhs — to cover treatment cost and 2 years income replacement.

💡 Already have Health Insurance? Critical illness works differently — health insurance reimburses hospital bills; critical illness gives a lump sum regardless of actual bills. Both are needed for complete financial protection. Agent explains how to combine both optimally.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Minimal documentation needed. Agent helps compile everything and submits digitally to insurer for fastest policy issuance.

👤 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (identity + address)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • 2 recent passport size photographs
  • Bank cancelled cheque (for auto-debit)

🏥 Medical & Health Documents

  • Recent medical test reports (if any existing conditions)
  • Past hospitalisation records (if applicable)
  • Existing health/life insurance policy details
  • Doctor prescriptions for ongoing medications
  • Medical examination report (if required by insurer)

💼 Income & Financial Documents

  • Last 3 months salary slips (salaried)
  • Last 2 years ITR / Form 16 (for high sum insured)
  • Business income proof (self-employed)
  • Bank statements — last 3 months

📋 Proposal & Nomination

  • Duly filled proposal form (agent helps fill)
  • Nominee's Aadhaar + PAN (for claim payout)
  • Nominee relationship proof (birth certificate, marriage certificate)
  • NACH mandate form (for premium auto-debit)

📱 All documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp. Agent will verify completeness and submit to insurer on your behalf.

Plan Benefits

Why Critical Illness Insurance is the smart choice

A serious illness doesn't just affect your health — it wipes out savings, disrupts income, and burdens family. This plan protects all three.

💵 Lump Sum — Use It Your Way

Unlike health insurance, CI pays a fixed lump sum on diagnosis. Use it for treatment, experimental therapy, overseas treatment, home modification, or debt repayment — no restrictions.

🏠 Covers Income Loss During Recovery

Critical illness treatment often means months away from work. Lump sum replaces lost income during the long recovery period — no EMI default, no family hardship.

💰 Section 80D Tax Benefit

Premium paid for self and family is 100% deductible under Section 80D — up to ₹25,000 for self/family, ₹50,000 if parents are senior citizens. Real savings every year.

🔓 Works Alongside Health Insurance

Health insurance pays the hospital bill. CI insurance pays YOU directly. Having both ensures zero out-of-pocket expenses even for the most expensive treatments like cancer chemotherapy.

🌍 Covers Overseas Treatment

Lump sum can be used for treatment at any hospital globally — including USA, UK, Singapore. No insurer approval needed for where you spend the claim amount.

📈 Affordable Premiums, High Value

₹25 Lakhs CI cover for a healthy 35-year-old costs approximately ₹500–₹700/month. That's less than a Netflix subscription protecting your family from financial ruin.
