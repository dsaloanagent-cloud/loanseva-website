Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Tractor Loan?

Farmers, agriculturists, and agri-entrepreneurs across AP & Telangana. Land holding is key — Agent will check your specific case.

🌾 Farmer Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Age

    18 to 70 years at time of loan application

  • 🏡

    Land Holding

    Minimum 1–2 acres agricultural land in applicant's name (varies by bank). RTC must be clear.

  • 📜

    Land Documents

    Valid Pattadar Passbook, RTC (Record of Rights), and Adangal — clear title required

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    650+ preferred. First-time borrowers (NTC) also considered with land as security.

  • 💰

    Income

    Seasonal agri income acceptable — bank understands crop cycle based repayment

💡 Small farmers (less than 2 acres)? Some NBFCs like Mahindra Finance and Cholamandalam consider smaller land holdings. Agent will check best available option for you.

🏢 Non-Farmer / Custom Hiring Eligibility

  • 🚜

    Custom Hiring

    Individuals running tractor hiring services / agri contracting business also eligible

  • 💼

    Business Proof

    Contract work records, past earnings, or existing equipment as proof of business

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700+ preferred for non-farmer applicants applying for commercial use

  • 💰

    Down Payment

    10–20% of tractor price as down payment required (varies by lender and tractor value)

💡 AP Rythu Bandhu & PM Kisan beneficiaries get priority processing with some banks — agent checks government scheme linkage benefits for you.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Tractor loan needs land documents — Agent helps collect and verify all at your doorstep. No bank visits needed.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs

🌾 Land & Agriculture Documents

  • Pattadar Passbook (mandatory)
  • RTC / Adangal — latest copy
  • Land survey / field map (if available)
  • Mutation records / link documents
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC)

💰 Income & Bank Documents

  • Last 12 months bank statement
  • Kisan Credit Card statement (if any)
  • PM Kisan / Rythu Bandhu passbook
  • Crop loan sanction letter (if existing)

🚜 Tractor / Dealer Documents

  • Tractor proforma invoice from dealer
  • Tractor quotation with model & price
  • Dealer registration certificate
  • RC & valuation report (used tractor)
  • Insurance copy (used tractor)

📱 All documents collected at your village / doorstep. Agent visits your home — no travel to city needed.
