Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for Individual Health Insurance?

Almost any individual can get health insurance — age, occupation, and health history influence the plan options and premium.

👤 Standard Individual Plans

  • 🎂

    Age

    18 to 65 years at entry (some plans up to 75+ years). Children from 90 days on separate plans.

  • 🏥

    Medical Underwriting

    Plans available without medical test up to age 45 for standard sum insured (varies by insurer).

  • 📊

    Pre-existing Conditions

    Conditions like diabetes or hypertension can be covered after a waiting period of 2–4 years.

  • 🏠

    Residence

    Any individual residing in Andhra Pradesh or Telangana is eligible for policy issuance.

💡 Senior citizens (60+) have dedicated plans with higher premiums but with full coverage — agent will suggest best plan based on your current health status and budget.

💼 Occupation-Specific & Special Cases

  • 👔

    Salaried Employees

    Even with company group insurance, individual top-up or super top-up plans are recommended for extra coverage.

  • 🏪

    Self-Employed / Business Owners

    No employer health cover — individual plans are essential and premiums are tax deductible under Section 80D.

  • 🎓

    Students

    Affordable plans with basic hospitalization coverage are available for students aged 18 and above.

  • 🌾

    Farmers & Rural Residents

    Plans with network hospitals in district and taluk-level towns are available — agent verifies network coverage in your area.

💡 Section 80D tax benefit — Premium paid for self, spouse, children: up to ₹25,000 deduction. Premium for parents: additional ₹25,000–₹50,000 (if senior citizen).

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Health insurance requires minimal documentation — mostly KYC and basic health declaration. Agent helps fill the forms.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID / Driving Licence / Passport
  • Recent utility bill or bank statement

🎂 Age Proof

  • Birth certificate
  • Class 10 / SSC marksheet
  • Passport (shows DOB)
  • Aadhaar (acceptable for most insurers)

🏥 Medical / Health Documents

  • Filled health declaration form (provided by insurer)
  • Recent medical reports (if pre-existing conditions)
  • Previous policy documents (for portability)
  • Medical test report (required above 45 for some plans)

📸 Photographs & Payment

  • 1 recent passport-size photograph
  • Cancelled cheque / bank details for ECS
  • Premium payment (online / UPI / cheque)
  • Nominee details with relationship proof

📱 Documents can be shared digitally via WhatsApp — agent handles form-filling and insurer coordination from start to policy issuance.

Coverage & Plan Options

Which plan type is right for you?

Different health insurance plan structures suit different needs — agent helps identify the best match.

🏥

Basic Hospitalization Plan

Covers in-patient hospitalization, room rent, ICU, surgeon, doctor fees. Best for first-time buyers on a budget.

🔬

Comprehensive Health Plan

All hospitalization benefits plus OPD, daycare procedures, pre- and post-hospitalization, annual health check-up.

🔝

Super Top-Up Plan

Kicks in after a deductible threshold — ideal supplement over existing employer cover or basic plan for extra protection.

💊

Critical Illness Add-On

Lump sum payout on diagnosis of cancer, heart attack, stroke, and other listed critical illnesses. Can be added as rider.

🧓

Senior Citizen Plan

Specially designed for individuals above 60 — higher premiums but covers age-related conditions with lower exclusions.

🔄

Policy Portability

Switch from your existing insurer to a better plan without losing waiting period credit — agent handles the entire porting process.

Premium, Sum Insured & Policy Details

What can you expect on your health plan?

Indicative figures — agent gives exact premium comparison after reviewing your age, health, and sum insured preference.

💵 Indicative Premiums

  • Age 25, ₹5L cover: ~₹6,000–₹9,000/yr
  • Age 35, ₹10L cover: ~₹10,000–₹16,000/yr
  • Age 45, ₹10L cover: ~₹18,000–₹28,000/yr
  • Age 60+, ₹5L cover: ~₹25,000–₹45,000/yr

🏥 In-Patient Hospitalization

  • Room rent: single AC or 1% of sum insured/day
  • ICU charges: 2% of sum insured/day typically
  • Surgeon, anaesthesia, specialist fees covered
  • Ambulance charges: usually ₹1,000–₹3,000

📋 Key Inclusions

  • Pre-hospitalization: 30–60 days
  • Post-hospitalization: 60–90 days
  • Daycare procedures: 500+ listed
  • AYUSH treatment (Ayurveda/Homeopathy)

🚫 Common Exclusions

  • Cosmetic / plastic surgery (unless accidental)
  • Self-inflicted injuries or substance abuse
  • Infertility / IVF treatments
  • War, nuclear hazards, experimental treatments
