OD vs Cash Credit

Overdraft & Cash Credit — difference emi?

Rendu revolving credit facilities — kani different purposes ki designed chesaru. Meeru business type batti agent right option suggest chestadu.

🏧 Overdraft (OD) Salaried / Property Based

Savings account or current account against sanctioned credit limit. Property, FD, or salary against OD possible. Withdraw anytime, interest only on used amount.

  • Current / savings account ki linked — same account lo withdraw & repay
  • Property (LAP), FD, shares, salary against OD possible
  • Salaried professionals ki salary OD — 2–3 months salary limit
  • No fixed repayment schedule — interest monthly deducted
  • Limit ₹50,000 nundi ₹5 Crore varakai (collateral based)
  • Rate: 9.5% – 16% p.a. (collateral type based)

🏢 Cash Credit (CC) Business / Trading

Working capital facility specifically for businesses — stock, debtors, receivables against CC limit. Most popular form of business credit in India.

  • Dedicated CC account — separate from current account
  • Stock (inventory) & book debts (receivables) as primary security
  • Trading, manufacturing, distribution businesses ki ideal
  • Drawing power based on monthly stock & debtors statements
  • Limit ₹5 Lakhs nundi ₹5 Crore varakai (turnover & stock based)
  • Rate: 10% – 16% p.a. (business profile based)

Eligibility Criteria

OD / Cash Credit ki enta eligibility kavali?

Business turnover, collateral, credit history — key factors. Agent profile review chesi best option identify chestadu.

🏧 Overdraft (OD) Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 65 years. Salaried ki 21–58, self-employed / business ki 23–65.

  • 🏠

    Collateral / Security

    Property (residential/commercial), FD, NSC, Shares, LIC policy, or salary (for salaried OD).

  • 💰

    Income / Salary

    Salaried OD ki minimum ₹25,000/month net salary. Property OD ki stable income proof.

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    680+ preferred. Clean repayment history, no defaults or NPA accounts.

💡 FD unte guaranteed OD! Fixed deposit against OD — 80–90% of FD value instantly available. No income proof needed, approval same day. Agent process chestadu.

🏢 Cash Credit (CC) Eligibility

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 2 years in business with regular operations and consistent bank transactions.

  • 💰

    Annual Turnover

    Minimum ₹30 Lakhs annual turnover for banks. CC limit typically 20–25% of annual turnover.

  • 📝

    GST & ITR Filing

    Active GST filing mandatory. Last 2 years ITR required. CA-audited financials for higher limits.

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700+ preferred for CC. Both personal & business CIBIL checked. No settled accounts.

💡 High turnover but low CIBIL? Some PSU banks (SBI, Canara) focus more on turnover & stock statements than CIBIL for CC. Agent best bank identify chestadu meeru profile ki.
