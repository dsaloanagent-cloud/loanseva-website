Eligibility Criteria

Who can buy a ULIP plan?

ULIPs are available to a wide range of individuals. Basic eligibility depends on age, health, and income — agent confirms exact criteria for each insurer.

👤 Individual Buyers

  • 🎂

    Age

    18 to 65 years at entry (maturity age up to 75–80 years depending on insurer)

  • 💼

    Income / Employment

    Salaried, self-employed, business owners, and professionals all eligible

  • 🏥

    Health Status

    Good health required; minor medical conditions may need declaration or higher premium

  • 📊

    PAN Card

    PAN mandatory for premium above ₹50,000/year as per IRDAI and tax regulations

💡 NRIs and PIOs can also buy ULIPs subject to FEMA guidelines and specific insurer policies — agent will confirm which plans are NRI-friendly.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family & Special Categories

  • 👶

    Child ULIPs

    Child policies available from age 0 – life assured is parent, child is beneficiary

  • 👴

    Senior Buyers

    Some insurers offer ULIPs up to entry age 60–65 with reduced sum assured options

  • 🏢

    Business Owners

    Keyman ULIP and group ULIP options available for business and employee benefit planning

  • 👩

    Women Applicants

    Some insurers offer lower premium rates for women policyholders — agent compares these

💡 Joint life ULIPs cover two lives under a single plan — ideal for couples planning retirement or child's future together.

Benefits

Why a ULIP is a smart financial choice

ULIPs uniquely combine life insurance protection with market-linked wealth creation — and generous tax benefits on both ends.

📉 Tax Savings under 80C

Premium paid up to ₹1.5 Lakhs per year is fully deductible under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act — reduces your taxable income.

🎁 Tax-Free Maturity under 10(10D)

If sum assured is at least 10× annual premium, maturity proceeds are completely tax-free under Section 10(10D) — a major advantage over MFs.

🛡️ Life Insurance Cover Included

Family gets sum assured (at least 10× annual premium) in case of policyholder's death — life protection without a separate policy.

📈 Market-Linked Growth

Long-term equity fund performance in ULIPs has historically delivered 10–14%* annualised returns — far higher than traditional endowment plans.

🔄 Fund Flexibility

Switch between equity, debt, and balanced funds anytime (4+ free switches/year) — adapt to market cycles and changing risk appetite.

💵 Partial Withdrawal Option

After the 5-year lock-in, partial withdrawals are allowed for emergencies — unlike PPF or traditional insurance plans. Tax-free too.

📅 Policy Term & Maturity

  • Recommended minimum: 10–15 years for equity
  • Lock-in: 5 years (IRDAI mandated)
  • Maturity: lump sum or settlement option
  • Free look period: 15–30 days after issuance
Tax Benefits & Policy Advantages

How the government rewards ULIP investors

ULIPs enjoy some of the best tax treatments among investment products under the Income Tax Act.

💰 Section 80C Deduction

Premium paid up to ₹1.5 Lakhs per financial year is deductible from gross taxable income under Section 80C — saves tax every year you pay premium.

Up to ₹1.5L deduction/year

🎁 Section 10(10D) — Tax-Free Maturity

Maturity proceeds are fully exempt from income tax under Section 10(10D), provided sum assured is at least 10× annual premium — unlike mutual funds.

100% tax-free returns

🛡️ Death Benefit — Tax-Free

Death benefit received by nominee is fully tax-free in the hands of the beneficiary under Section 10(10D) — no income tax on claim amount whatsoever.

Tax-free death claim

🔄 Switching — No Capital Gains Tax

Switching between equity and debt funds within a ULIP does not attract capital gains tax — a significant advantage over switching between mutual fund schemes.

Zero tax on fund switch

📋 Tax laws are subject to change. Agent provides guidance based on current Income Tax Act provisions — consult a tax advisor for personalised tax planning.
