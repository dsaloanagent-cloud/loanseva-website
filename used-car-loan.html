Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Used car loans need both applicant documents and vehicle documents. Agent tells you exactly what's needed for your chosen lender.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Utility bill for address proof

💰 Income Documents

  • Last 3 months salary slips (salaried)
  • Last 6 months bank statement
  • Last 2 years ITR (self-employed)
  • Form 16 / CA certificate if applicable

🚗 Vehicle Documents

  • RC Book (Registration Certificate) copy
  • Current valid insurance certificate
  • Seller's NOC / Sale agreement
  • Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate

📋 Additional Documents

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Signed loan application form
  • Original RC for verification by bank
  • Service history / maintenance records (if any)
