Comparison Benefits

Why comparing insurance is the smartest move

The right insurance plan saves money at purchase and at claim time. Comparison makes the difference.

💸 Save Up to 40% on Premium

Same sum insured, same coverage — different insurers charge 20–40% different premiums. A 5-minute comparison can save ₹2,000–₹15,000/year on health insurance alone.

✅ Better Claim Settlement Ratio

Agent helps you avoid insurers with poor claim history. Comparing CSR data ensures claim doesn't get rejected when you need it most.

🏥 More Network Hospitals

Health insurance with higher network hospitals near your home = easier cashless treatment. Comparison shows which insurer covers your nearest hospital.

⏳ Lower Waiting Periods

Pre-existing disease waiting period varies from 1–4 years across insurers. Agent identifies plans with shortest waiting period relevant to your health conditions.

➕ Right Add-on Covers

OPD cover, critical illness, maternity, zero depreciation — some plans include these, some charge extra. Comparison ensures you only pay for what you actually need.

🔄 Portability Knowledge

Already have insurance but unhappy? Agent checks if porting to a better insurer is worth it — with continuity benefits and no waiting period reset for pre-existing diseases.

Claim Settlement Ratio (CSR) — insurance company total claims received lo enni successfully settled chesindo percentage. IRDA every year annual report lo publish chestundi. 95%+ unte excellent, 90–95% good, 85–90% average, below 85% — avoid. Example: Niva Bupa 96.5% means 100 claims lo 96.5 settle chesaaru. Higher = better protection for you at claim time.
Multiple factors premium determine chestaayi: policyholder age, health conditions, occupation, location, add-on covers, waiting period length, network hospital size, claim settlement history, insurer's own risk assessment models. Same ₹10L health cover ki company A ₹12,000 charge chestundi, company B ₹8,000 — but company B ki fewer network hospitals or higher waiting period untundi. Comparison tool idi anni explain chestundi.
Very important! Network hospitals lo cashless treatment vastaadi — hospital bill meeru pay cheyyadam ledu, insurer directly hospital ki pay chestundi. Non-network lo reimbursement claim pettali — advance payment cheyyi, taruvata claim file cheyyi, wait cheyyi. AP & TG lo — Star Health 1,200+ hospitals, HDFC Ergo 1,100+, Niva Bupa 850+ network hospitals unnaayi. Meeru nearest hospital check cheyyadam important — agent mee city ki specific list ista.
Waiting period — policy start ayyina taruvata certain period ki claim file cheyyaleru. Types: Initial waiting period (30–90 days, accidents except), Pre-existing disease waiting (1–4 years), Specific disease waiting (cataracts, hernia — 1–2 years). Lower waiting period = better policy. Niva Bupa Reassure 2.0 ki PED waiting only 1 year — most others 2–3 years. Agent comparison lo idi clearly highlight chestadu.

Family floater: single sum insured entire family share chestundi — cost effective when multiple members use it.
