Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for an MSME Loan?

Existing businesses ki, new ventures ki — wide eligibility. Udyam registration and basic financials unte loan possible.

🏭 Existing Business (2+ Years)

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 1–2 years operational; some banks allow 6 months with strong GST turnover

  • 📊

    Annual Turnover

    Minimum ₹10L–₹25L annual turnover as per GST returns / bank statement

  • 🆔

    Udyam Registration

    Udyam certificate mandatory for most MSME schemes (free registration — agent helps)

  • 📋

    CIBIL Score

    650+ preferred; NBFC options available for 600+ with strong business cash flow

💡 GST registered businesses get faster processing and higher loan amounts. GST returns 6–12 months strong unte bank approval very easy avutuundi.

🚀 New / Startup Business

  • 🎂

    Owner Age

    21 to 65 years; women entrepreneurs get preferential rates from most banks

  • 📝

    Business Plan

    Detailed project report (DPR) required for PMEGP and new venture loans above ₹10L

  • 🎓

    Qualification

    No minimum qualification for Mudra loans; trade / ITI certificate helpful for manufacturing

  • 🏠

    Personal CIBIL

    650+ owner CIBIL; clean credit history helps. First-time borrowers considered under govt schemes

💡 Women entrepreneurs, SC/ST, OBC, minorities get extra subsidy and priority under PMEGP, Stand-Up India, and state government schemes. Agent checks all applicable benefits.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

MSME loans need business documents along with personal ID proof. Agent gives exact checklist based on loan type and lender selected.

🆔 Owner Identity & Address

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card — personal + business
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Utility bill / rental agreement for address

🏭 Business Registration Proof

  • Udyam Registration Certificate
  • GST Registration Certificate
  • Shop & Establishment licence
  • Partnership deed / MOA / AOA (if applicable)

💰 Financial Documents

  • Last 12 months business bank statement
  • Last 2 years ITR with computation
  • Last 2 years GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B)
  • Audited balance sheet / P&L (if above ₹25L)

📋 Additional Documents

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Project report / business plan (new ventures)
  • Quotations for machinery / equipment
  • Existing loan statements (if any)

📱 Documents WhatsApp lo submit cheyavachu — Agent loan type and lender batti exact checklist share chestadu. Udyam registration lekapoite memu register cheyyadaniki help chestaam.

Government MSME Schemes

Special Government Schemes for MSME Businesses

Central and state government numerous schemes provide. Meeru eligible scheme identify chesi maximum benefit teesukodaniki Agent help chestadu.

PM Mudra Yojana

🌸 Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana

Micro enterprises ki collateral-free loans. Shishu (up to ₹50K), Kishore (₹50K–5L), Tarun (₹5L–10L) — three categories. Non-farm income generating businesses ki applicable.

Loan Amount: Up to ₹10 Lakhs Collateral: Not Required Processing Fee: Nil (Shishu) Best For: Micro businesses, street vendors, artisans
CGTMSE Scheme

🛡️ Credit Guarantee Fund (CGTMSE)

Collateral-free loans up to ₹2 Crores tho bank guarantee. Government trust guarantee betti bank loan approval chestundi. New and existing MSMEs eligible.

Loan Amount: Up to ₹2 Crores Guarantee Coverage: 75%–85% Annual Fee: 0.37%–1.35% Best For: Small businesses needing collateral-free loans
PMEGP Scheme

🏗️ PM Employment Generation Programme

New enterprises setup ki project cost meda 15–35% capital subsidy. Manufacturing ₹25L, service ₹10L project cost ki applicable. Rural areas ki higher subsidy.

Project Cost: Up to ₹50 Lakhs (Mfg) Subsidy: 15–35% of project cost Own Contribution: 5–10% Best For: New manufacturing / service businesses
Stand-Up India

🌟 Stand-Up India Scheme

SC/ST and women entrepreneurs ki ₹10L – ₹1Cr greenfield enterprise setup loan. Each bank branch mandatorily 1 SC/ST and 1 women borrower serve cheyali.

Loan Amount: ₹10 Lakhs – ₹1 Crore Eligible: SC/ST + Women entrepreneurs Tenure: Up to 7 years Best For: First-time women / SC/ST entrepreneurs
PM SVANidhi

🛺 PM Street Vendor AtmaNirbhar Nidhi

Street vendors ki ₹10,000 – ₹50,000 collateral-free working capital loan. Regular repayment taaruvata higher loan eligibility. AP & TG lo widely available.

Loan Amount: ₹10,000 – ₹50,000 Collateral: Not Required Interest Subsidy: 7% per annum Best For: Street vendors, small traders
SIDBI Schemes

🏦 SIDBI Direct / Indirect Lending

Small Industries Development Bank of India — MSME ki specialized loans. SMILE (SIDBI Make in India Soft Loan Fund), equipment finance, digital lending available.

Loan Amount: ₹10 Lakhs – ₹25 Crores Tenure: Up to 10 years Rate: Competitive linked rates Best For: Mid-scale manufacturing and services
