Eligibility Criteria

Basic eligibility for applying

Each service ki specific eligibility varies — below general guidelines. Agent mee exact profile batti exact eligibility confirm chestādu.

🎂

Age

18 to 65 years for most loans. Pension loans up to 75 years. Insurance up to 70 years entry age. Age batti product selection avutundi.

💼

Employment Status

Salaried, self-employed, business owners, farmers, pensioners — all eligible. Loan type varies per employment type. Agent matches right product.

💰

Minimum Income

Personal Loan: ₹15,000/month min. Home Loan: ₹20,000+. Business Loan: Turnover based. Insurance: No income requirement.

📊

CIBIL Score

700+ preferred for personal/home loans. Some banks 650+ okay with collateral. Low CIBIL unte NBFC options available. Agent guides based on actual score.

🏠

Residency

AP & Telangana residents priority. Residents of other states with property in AP/TG also eligible. NRI options available for select products.

🏥

Health (Insurance)

Health insurance: basic health declaration required. Pre-existing conditions: group policies cover from day 1. Individual policies have waiting periods.

Required Documents
