Eligibility Criteria

Who can buy a Whole Life Insurance Policy?

Wide eligibility — young professionals nundi senior citizens varaku. Age and health condition ki suitable plans available. Earlier buy chesthe premium takkuva avutuundi.

👔 Standard Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Entry Age

    Minimum 18 years; maximum 55–65 years depending on insurer and plan chosen

  • ❤️

    Health Condition

    Standard health — no major pre-existing illness. Medical tests may be required for high sum assured

  • 🆔

    Identity

    Indian citizen with valid Aadhaar and PAN. NRIs eligible in select plans with additional documents

  • 💰

    Income Proof

    For high sum assured (₹50L+) — income documents required to establish insurability

💡 Age 18–35 lo buy chesthe premium chala takkuva avutuundi. Whole life lo 25 years premium pay chesthe, remaining 60+ years coverage FREE. Younger age lo buy cheyyadam best investment decision.

🏥 Health & Special Cases

  • 🩺

    Medical Exam

    Sum assured above ₹25L–₹50L ki medical tests mandatory; below threshold — declaration only

  • 💊

    Pre-existing Conditions

    Diabetes, hypertension, heart conditions — higher premium (loading) tho coverage possible in most cases

  • 🚭

    Smoker / Non-Smoker

    Non-smokers get lower premiums; smoker rates slightly higher. Declaration required at time of proposal

  • 👶

    Child Plans

    LIC and some insurers allow whole life plans for children from age 0 with parent as proposer

💡 Health issues unaanaandatiki worry cheyyadam ledu — Agent meeru health profile check chesi accepted plan, loading amount, and best insurer identify chestadu. Rejection minimize cheyyadaniki right insurer match important.
