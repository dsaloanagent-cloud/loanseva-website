Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Study Abroad Loan?

Student + parent / guardian co-applicant tho apply cheyali. Admission confirmation unnante loan fast avutuundi.

🎓 Student Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Age

    18 to 35 years; Indian citizen with valid passport

  • 📚

    Academic Record

    Minimum 50–60% in last qualifying examination (10+2 or graduation)

  • 🏛️

    Admission

    Confirmed admission letter from recognized foreign university / institution

  • 📝

    Entrance Tests

    GRE / GMAT / IELTS / TOEFL scores (as required by the program)

💡 Apply before admission? Conditional loan sanction letter vastaadi — university application process ki use cheyavachu. Admission confirm ayyaka final disbursal avutuundi.

👨‍👩‍👧 Co-Applicant (Parent / Guardian)

  • 💰

    Income

    Salaried: ₹3L+/yr; Self-employed: ITR ₹3L+ for last 2 years

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700+ preferred for co-applicant; affects interest rate offered

  • 🏠

    Collateral (above ₹50L)

    Property, FD, or LIC policy as security for loans above ₹50 Lakhs

  • 🤝

    Relationship

    Parent, sibling, or spouse can be co-applicant; spouse income also counted

💡 No collateral? NBFC lenders — Avanse, HDFC Credila, InCred — unsecured loans up to ₹75L for top-ranked universities and strong profiles. Agent checks options.
