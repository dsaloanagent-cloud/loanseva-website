Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply for a Business Credit Card?

Business owners, self-employed professionals, MSME operators, private companies — wide eligibility. Income proof, CIBIL, and business vintage key factors.

🏢 Business Owner / Self-Employed

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 65 years; principal applicant (business owner / director / partner)

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 1–2 years operational business; GST registration preferred

  • 💰

    Annual Turnover

    Minimum ₹12 Lakhs annual business income as per ITR or bank statement

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700+ preferred for best cards; 650+ for entry-level business cards

💡 GST registered businesses get faster approvals and higher limits. Regular GST filer tho ITR filed unaandataniki best cards approve avutuundi. Agent pre-check chestadu rejection avoid cheyyadaniki.

🏭 Private Ltd / Partnership / LLP

  • 📝

    Business Entity

    Proprietorships, partnerships, LLPs, private limited companies — all eligible for business cards

  • 💼

    Director / Partner

    Card issued in director or authorized signatory name — company account linked

  • 📈

    Company Turnover

    Company financials consider chestaayi — Pvt Ltd ki audited balance sheet + P&L required

  • 🏦

    Banking Relationship

    Existing bank customer ki higher limit and better terms — salary, current, or savings account tho

💡 Professionals — Doctors, CAs, Lawyers, Architects — dedicated professional business cards available with income-based eligibility. Practice vintage 2+ years unte easily qualify. Agent correct card category recommend chestadu.
