Compare Interest Rates, EMI & Processing Fees

How small rate differences add up

A lower headline rate isn't always the full picture — processing fee and tenure change your real cost too.

📉 What Affects Your Interest Rate

  • 📊

    Credit Score

    Higher CIBIL score (750+) typically unlocks the lowest available rates

  • 💼

    Income & Employment Type

    Salaried with stable income often gets better rates than self-employed

  • 🏦

    Lender Type

    PSU banks usually offer lower rates than NBFCs, but NBFCs approve faster

  • 🔗

    Existing Relationship

    Existing salary/savings account holders sometimes get preferential rates

💰 What Affects Processing Fee

  • 📋

    Loan Category

    Secured loans (home/property) often have lower fee % than unsecured personal loans

  • 🎯

    Promotional Offers

    Banks periodically run "zero processing fee" campaigns — agent tracks these for you

  • 📈

    Loan Amount Slabs

    Some lenders charge a flat fee below a threshold, percentage-based above it

  • 🔄

    Negotiation Scope

    Processing fee is often more negotiable than the interest rate itself

💡 Always check total repayment, not just EMI — a slightly higher EMI over a shorter tenure can mean lakhs less in total interest paid.
