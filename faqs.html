EMI Calculator & Eligibility FAQs

EMI calculation, CIBIL score, loan eligibility, interest rate questions

750+ — excellent, best rates, fast approval. 700–750 — good, slight higher rate. 650–700 — some banks, higher rate. 600–650 — NBFCs only, much higher rate. Below 600 — very limited, very high rate. Regular payments on time + low credit utilisation (below 30%) + no multiple enquiries — CIBIL improve chestaayi 6–12 months lo. Agent free CIBIL check guide chestadu. EMI Calculator: ikkade use cheyavachu.
EMI formula: P × R × (1+R)^N / [(1+R)^N – 1]. Tenure increase chesthe EMI takkuva but total interest more. Rate 1% takkuva chesthe EMI takkuva + total savings significant. Example: ₹20L loan — 20yr@9% = ₹17,996 EMI, 20yr@8% = ₹16,729 EMI — ₹1,267 monthly savings. Online EMI Calculator tool try cheyyi — different scenarios compare cheyyi.
Standard rule: total monthly EMI (all loans) ≤ 40–50% of net monthly income (FOIR). Example: net income ₹50,000 ki — max EMI ₹20,000–₹25,000. Existing car loan EMI ₹5,000 unaandataniki — new loan ki available EMI ₹15,000–₹20,000. ₹20L loan@9%@20yr = ₹18,000 EMI — eligible. Agent exact eligibility amount calculate chestadu meeru income, existing EMIs, and credit profile batti.
600 → 700: approximately 6–12 months with consistent on-time payments. 700 → 750+: 12–18 months. Steps: (1) All EMIs, credit card bills on time pay cheyyi. (2) Credit utilisation 30% below maintain cheyyi. (3) Old closed accounts don't close — history length matters. (4) Multiple new applications avoid cheyyi. (5) Mix of credit (loan + card) beneficial. Agent credit health assessment free gaa provide chestadu.
Fixed rate: EMI predictable, rate hike risk ledu, typically 0.5–1% higher than floating. Floating rate: RBI repo rate tho link — rate cuts ki benefitavutuundi, slight uncertainty. Long term (home loan) lo — floating historically beneficial — rate cycles avutuundi. Short term (personal loan, car loan) lo — fixed preferred for predictability. Agent market rate trend analyze chesi recommend chestadu.

Example: income ₹80,000, existing EMI ₹20,000 — available EMI capacity ₹20,000–₹24,000 — additional loan possible. Good income, good CIBIL unaandataniki multiple loans manage cheyavachu. Agent exact eligibility check chestadu.
Total Cost of Loan = Interest + Processing fee + Other charges. Low rate + high fee vs high rate + zero fee — calculate chesi compare cheyali. Example: ₹10L loan, 0.5% fee = ₹5,000 one-time vs 0.25% higher rate = extra ₹6,000–₹8,000 over 5 years — rate matters more. Short loans ki fee matters more, long loans ki rate more important.
