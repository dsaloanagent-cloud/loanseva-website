DSA Loan Agent Ante Emiti — Quick Recap

DSA (Direct Selling Agent) ante bank/NBFC తరపున customers ki loan products connect chese oka registered partner. Meeru bank branch ki nేరుగా వెళ్ళకుండా, agent dwara meeru loan apply cheyachu — process same or fast, kani meeku 30+ bank options compare చేసే advantage extra ga vastundi.

DSA Loan Agent service lo, customer ki nేరుగా loan ivvamu — meeru banks/NBFCs కి connect చేసి, meeku best-fit lender ఎంపిక చేయడంలో help chestham. Actual loan sanction & disbursal bank/NBFC dwara jarugutundi.

💡 Important: DSA Loan Agent evariki nేరుగా loan ivvadu. Manam bank/NBFC ki మీ application ni సరైన rīti లో సమర్పించి, follow-up చేసి, fast approval తీసుకురావడానికి సహాయపడతాము.

Full Process — Enquiry Nunchi Disbursal Varaku

1

Enquiry Submit

Call, WhatsApp, leda website form dwara meeru loan requirement cheppandi — type, amount, purpose.

2

Free Eligibility Check

Agent meeru salary/income, CIBIL score, existing loans anni చూసి, meeku ఏ bank best fit ani analyze chestadu — CIBIL మీద ప్రభావం ఉండదు.

3

Bank/Lender Selection

30+ banks & NBFCs లో, meeru approve అయ్యే chance ekkuvunna 2-3 lenders shortlist chestadu.

4

Document Collection

Aadhaar, PAN, income proof, bank statements — digital ga (WhatsApp/email) leda doorstep collection dwara సేకరిస్తాడు.

5

Application Submission

Complete, error-free application ni selected bank ki submit chestadu — missing documents valla reject avvakunda ensure chestadu.

6

Bank Processing & Follow-up

Bank internal verification, credit check chesthundi. Agent daily follow-up chesi status update istadu.

7

Sanction Letter

Bank loan approve chesi sanction letter ఇస్తుంది — terms, interest rate, tenure confirm avutundi.

8

Disbursal

Final agreement sign chesaka, loan amount నేరుగా మీ bank account కి (leda seller/builder కి, home loan అయితే) credit avutundi.

Loan Reject Aithe Emi Avutundi?

Konni సందర్భాల్లో, ఒక bank meeru profile ki match కాకపోతే loan reject cheyవచ్చు — తక్కువ CIBIL, income mismatch, or property issue వల్ల. Ee situation lo agent:

  • Reject కావడానికి exact reason identify chestadu
  • Meeru profile ki match aina different bank/NBFC ki dubara try chestadu
  • CIBIL improve చేయడానికి, leda income proof strengthen చేయడానికి guidance istadu

Ee process ki additional charge ఏమీ ఉండదు — DSA Loan Agent meeru successful ga loan తీసుకునే వరకు support చేస్తుంది.
