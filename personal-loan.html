Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Personal Loan?

Basic criteria to get personal loan approved. Low CIBIL? Don't worry — we have lenders for that too.

👔 Salaried Employees

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 60 years at loan maturity

  • 💼

    Employment

    Minimum 1 year total experience, 6 months with current employer

  • 💰

    Minimum Salary

    ₹15,000/month (private) or ₹10,000/month (government)

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    680 or above preferred. Lower scores also considered with some lenders.

💡 Low CIBIL? We work with NBFCs like Bajaj Finance & Poonawalla that approve loans for scores as low as 600. Agent will guide you to the right lender.

🏢 Self-Employed / Business Owners

  • 🎂

    Age

    25 to 65 years at loan maturity

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 2 years of business operation with profit

  • 💰

    Annual Income

    Minimum ₹2.5 Lakhs annual income (ITR basis)

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700 or above preferred for self-employed category

💡 No ITR filed? Some NBFCs offer loans based on bank statement analysis. Agent will check your best available option.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Minimal paperwork — most documents you already have. We collect them digitally or from your doorstep.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Utility bill (electricity/water) for address

💼 Income Proof — Salaried

  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Last 6 months bank statement
  • Form 16 / latest ITR
  • Employment ID / Offer letter

🏢 Income Proof — Self Employed

  • Last 2 years ITR with computation
  • Last 12 months bank statement
  • Business registration proof (GST/Udyam)
  • Balance sheet / P&L statement

📸 Photographs & Others

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Signed application form
  • Existing loan statements (if any)
  • Office address proof (self-employed)

📱 All documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp — no branch visit needed.

Loan Benefits

Why a Personal Loan makes sense

Flexible, fast, and no strings attached — personal loans are India's most popular financial tool.

🔓 No Collateral Required

You don't need to pledge your property, gold, or any asset. Purely based on your income and credit profile.

🎯 Use For Any Purpose

Medical emergency, wedding, home renovation, travel, education fees, debt consolidation — no restrictions on use.

📆 Flexible Tenure

Choose repayment period from 12 to 60 months based on your comfort. Lower tenure = less total interest.

💳 Fixed EMI Structure

Know exactly what you'll pay every month — no surprises. Fixed EMI makes budgeting simple and predictable.
