Eligibility Criteria

Who can do a Home Loan Balance Transfer?

If you have an active home loan with any bank, balance transfer is usually possible. Agent will verify your specific case for free.

✅ Basic BT Eligibility

  • 🏦

    Existing Home Loan

    Active home loan with any bank, NBFC, or HFC — at least 12 EMIs paid regularly

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700 or above preferred. Clean repayment track record essential.

  • 💰

    Outstanding Amount

    Minimum ₹5 Lakhs outstanding — smaller amounts may not justify processing costs

  • 📅

    Remaining Tenure

    10+ years remaining recommended to maximise interest savings post-transfer

  • 🏗️

    Property Status

    Property should be legally clear, construction complete / near completion

💡 Rate difference matters: BT is most beneficial when new rate is at least 0.5% lower than current rate. Agent calculates your exact saving before recommending.

🚫 When BT May NOT Be Worth It

  • ⚠️

    Very Low Outstanding

    Less than ₹5 Lakhs outstanding — processing costs may exceed interest saving

  • ⚠️

    Short Remaining Tenure

    Less than 5 years remaining — saving is too small to justify transfer effort and fee

  • ⚠️

    Fixed Rate Loan

    If your current loan is on fixed rate — check foreclosure charges first. May be costly.

  • ⚠️

    Low Rate Difference

    Rate difference less than 0.25% — saving may not exceed new bank processing fee

💡 Not sure if BT is right for you? Agent does a free savings vs cost analysis — meeru informed decision teesukogalavacchu. No commitment needed.

Required Documents

Documents needed for Balance Transfer

BT documents are similar to a fresh home loan — plus a few extras from your existing bank. Agent guides and collects everything.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Utility bill for current address proof

💼 Income Proof

  • Last 3 months salary slips (salaried)
  • Last 12 months bank statement
  • Last 2–3 years ITR (self-employed)
  • Form 16 / CA certified financials

🏦 Existing Loan Documents

  • Existing bank loan account statement (12 months)
  • Sanction letter from current bank
  • NOC / Foreclosure letter from current bank
  • List of original documents held by current bank

🏗️ Property Documents

  • Original sale deed / title documents
  • EC (Encumbrance Certificate) — latest
  • Building approval / layout plan
  • Property tax receipts (latest)
  • NOC from society / builder (if applicable)

📱 Agent handles NOC collection from your old bank — one of the trickiest parts. We know the process well.
