Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Home Loan?

Salaried, self-employed, business owners — almost everyone with stable income is eligible. Agent will check your best option.

👔 Salaried Employees

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 65 years at loan maturity

  • 💼

    Employment

    Minimum 2 years total, 6 months with current employer

  • 💰

    Minimum Salary

    ₹20,000/month net salary (varies by bank)

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    700 or above preferred. NTC (new to credit) cases also considered.

  • 🏗️

    Property

    Clear title, approved layout / RERA registered property preferred

💡 Government employees (central / state / PSU) get preferential rates and higher loan amounts from SBI, PNB, and Canara Bank.

🏢 Self-Employed / Business Owners

  • 🎂

    Age

    25 to 70 years at loan maturity

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 3 years of business with profit track record

  • 💰

    Annual Income

    Minimum ₹3 Lakhs annual (ITR basis). Higher income = higher loan.

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    720 or above preferred for self-employed category

  • 📋

    ITR Filed

    Last 2–3 years ITR mandatory for most banks

💡 No ITR or low ITR? Some banks and HFCs (Housing Finance Companies) offer loans based on bank statement assessment. Agent will guide you.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Home loan documentation is more detailed than personal loans — but we guide you through every step. Agent collects digitally or from your home.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Utility bill (electricity/water) — address

💼 Income Proof — Salaried

  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Last 12 months bank statement
  • Form 16 / last 2 years ITR
  • Employment ID / appointment letter

🏢 Income Proof — Self Employed

  • Last 3 years ITR with computation
  • Last 12 months bank statement
  • Business registration (GST/Udyam/COI)
  • CA certified balance sheet & P&L

🏗️ Property Documents

  • Sale agreement / sale deed
  • Property title documents (chain of title)
  • Building plan approval / layout approval
  • EC (Encumbrance Certificate)
  • RERA registration certificate
  • NOC from builder / society

📱 Documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp. Agent will also help with property legal & technical checklist.

Loan Benefits

Why a Home Loan makes complete sense

Owning your home is India's biggest financial decision — a home loan makes it achievable and tax-smart.

🏡 Own Your Dream Home Now

Don't wait 10–15 years to save. Home loan lets you buy today, live in it immediately, and repay comfortably over 20–30 years.

💸 Massive Tax Benefits

Section 80C: ₹1.5L deduction on principal. Section 24(b): ₹2L deduction on interest. Total savings up to ₹3.5L per year off your taxable income.

📈 Property Value Appreciation

Real estate in AP & Telangana growing at 8–12% annually. Property you buy today will be worth 2–3x in 10 years — loan repayment becomes even easier.

🔒 Long Tenure — Low EMI

Up to 30-year tenure keeps your monthly EMI low and comfortable. Example: ₹30L loan at 8.5% for 20 years = ₹26,035/month EMI only.

🔄 Zero Prepayment on Floating Rate

RBI mandates zero prepayment charges on floating rate home loans. Bonus income aa? Pay off early — zero penalty.

💳 Improves Credit Profile

Timely home loan EMI payments significantly boost your CIBIL score — making future financial needs (car, business, education) easier to fund.
