Group Health Insurance

What is Group Health Insurance and why does your business need it?

Group Health Insurance is a single policy that covers all employees of a company under one umbrella — providing medical protection at a fraction of individual policy costs. It is one of the most valued employee benefits any employer can offer.

🏢

For Every Business Size

Startups with 7 employees to large corporates with 500+ staff — group health insurance is available and affordable for all. Minimum group size varies by insurer.

💊

Day-1 PED Coverage

Unlike individual policies with 2–4 year waiting periods, group health insurance covers pre-existing diseases from the very first day of the policy — a massive benefit.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Coverage Option

Extend coverage to spouse, children, and dependent parents under a family floater add-on — making it a comprehensive benefit package for employees.

💰

Tax Deductible for Employers

Premiums paid towards employee group health insurance are 100% deductible as a business expense under Section 37(1) of the Income Tax Act.

🏥

Cashless Hospitalisation

Employees can avail cashless treatment at 10,000+ network hospitals — no upfront payment needed. Insurer settles directly with the hospital.

🤝

Employee Retention Tool

Health insurance is among the top 3 benefits employees consider when joining or staying at a company — a powerful retention and recruitment advantage.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can buy Group Health Insurance?

Private companies, NGOs, partnership firms, and even informal groups can avail group health insurance — eligibility is flexible across insurers.

🏢 Organisation Eligibility

  • 👥

    Minimum Group Size

    Generally 7 employees minimum — some insurers offer policies from 2–5 employees for micro businesses and startups

  • 🏛️

    Business Type

    Private limited, LLP, partnership, proprietorship, NGO, trust, educational institutions — all eligible

  • 📋

    GST / Company Registration

    Valid business registration proof required — GST certificate, company incorporation, shop act licence, etc.

  • 📊

    Industry Type

    IT, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, construction, hospitality — all industries covered; premium may vary by risk profile

💡 Startups and MSMEs with as few as 7 employees can get a genuine group health policy — agent finds the right insurer for small groups at competitive rates.

👤 Employee Eligibility

  • 🎂

    Employee Age

    Typically 18 to 65 years — some policies extend to 70 years for senior employees or working partners

  • 👨‍👩‍👧

    Family Members

    Spouse + up to 2–3 dependent children (up to age 25) + dependent parents — floater extension available

  • 🏥

    No Medical Tests

    No pre-policy medical examination required for groups up to 50–100 members in most insurer guidelines

  • 📅

    Joining Date

    New employees can be added mid-year on pro-rata premium basis — no waiting period for new joinees

💡 Contract staff and gig workers can also be included in some group policies — agent confirms eligibility based on employment relationship and chosen insurer rules.

Coverage & Employee Benefits

What does Group Health Insurance cover for your employees?

A well-designed group health policy covers far more than just hospitalisation — agent helps build the right benefit structure for your workforce.

🏥

Hospitalisation Cover

Room rent, surgeon fees, nursing charges, anaesthesia, medicines, diagnostics — all in-patient hospitalisation expenses covered up to sum insured.

💊

Pre-Existing Disease Cover

Diabetes, hypertension, thyroid, cardiac conditions — all pre-existing illnesses covered from policy day 1. No waiting period unlike individual policies.

🤰

Maternity & Newborn Cover

Normal delivery, C-section, pre & post natal expenses covered. Newborn covered from birth — popular add-on for companies with younger workforce.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Floater Extension

Spouse, children, and dependent parents covered under same sum insured or separate floater. Family protection as part of the employee benefit package.

🧪

Critical Illness Cover

Cancer, heart attack, kidney failure, stroke, organ transplant — optional critical illness top-up can be added for higher coverage for senior employees.

🦷

OPD, Dental & Vision

Out-patient consultations, dental treatment, spectacles, contact lenses — optional add-ons that significantly enhance the perceived value of the benefit.

Benefits of Group Health Insurance

Why Group Health Insurance is a smart business decision

Group health insurance is not just an employee benefit — it is a strategic business investment in your team's wellbeing, productivity, and loyalty.

💼 Attract & Retain Top Talent

Health insurance is the #1 benefit employees look for. Offering group mediclaim makes your company stand out — especially for young talent joining SMEs over large corporates.

💰 Lower Cost Than Individual Policies

Group rates are 30–50% cheaper than individual health policies for same coverage — because risk is pooled across all employees. Better value per rupee spent.

🧾 100% Tax Deductible Expense

Premium paid by employer towards group health insurance is fully deductible under Section 37(1) of the Income Tax Act — reduces your business tax liability directly.

🏥 Cashless Claims — No Disruption

Employees don't need to arrange funds during a medical emergency — cashless treatment at network hospitals ensures focus stays on recovery, not finances.

📈 Higher Productivity

Employees with health security have lower absenteeism and stress. Studies consistently show covered employees are 20–30% more productive than uncovered peers.

🛡️ Day-1 Pre-Existing Coverage

Unlike individual policies, group health insurance covers pre-existing conditions from day 1 — making it genuinely useful even for employees who need it most immediately.

Premium, Coverage & Claim Process

What to expect — premiums, limits & how claims work

Indicative premium and coverage ranges — agent gives exact quotes after employee census and coverage requirement discussion.

💰 Premium Ranges (Indicative)

  • ₹1L SI: ₹800 – ₹1,500 per employee/year
  • ₹2L SI: ₹1,500 – ₹2,800 per employee/year
  • ₹3–5L SI: ₹2,800 – ₹5,500 per employee/year
  • Family floater add: +40–60% on base premium

🏥 Key Coverage Limits

  • Sum Insured: ₹1L – ₹10L per employee
  • Room rent: single AC, ICU at actuals
  • Maternity limit: ₹25,000 – ₹1L (plan-based)
  • Pre & post hospitalisation: 30 + 60 days

🔧 Cashless Claim Process

  • Employee shows E-card at network hospital
  • Hospital raises pre-authorisation to insurer / TPA
  • TPA approves within 2–4 hours (emergencies faster)
  • Hospital treats, insurer pays directly — zero out-of-pocket

💸 Reimbursement Claim Process

  • Employee gets treated at any hospital
  • Submits original bills, discharge summary, prescriptions
  • TPA reviews and settles within 15–30 working days
  • Amount credited directly to employee bank account
