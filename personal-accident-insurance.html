Eligibility Criteria

Who can buy Personal Accident Insurance?

Any earning Indian resident between 5 and 70 years can get personal accident cover — no medical tests required for standard plans.

👤 Individual Eligibility

  • 🇮🇳

    Nationality

    Indian resident. NRI / foreigner residents in India also eligible with most insurers.

  • 🎂

    Entry Age

    5 to 70 years for most plans. Children from age 5 covered as dependents. Senior citizen plans up to 80 years available.

  • 💼

    Occupation

    All occupations eligible — office workers, labourers, drivers, farmers. Risk category (Class A/B/C) determines premium rate.

  • 🏥

    No Medical Test

    No pre-policy medical examination required for standard PA plans up to ₹1 Crore sum insured. Instant issuance.

  • 💰

    Income Basis

    Sum insured recommended at 10x annual income. Higher sum insured may require income proof above ₹50 Lakhs cover.

💡 High-Risk Occupation? Drivers, construction workers, miners, and labourers are classified as high-risk (Class C) — premium is higher but coverage is same. Agent identifies insurers with best rates for your occupation class.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Cover Eligibility

  • 👫

    Spouse Cover

    Spouse can be included in same policy at 50% of primary insured's sum insured — discounted premium.

  • 👶

    Children Cover

    Dependent children aged 5–25 years covered at 20–25% of primary sum insured. Students also eligible.

  • 👴

    Parents Cover

    Dependent parents can be included — entry age up to 70 years. Separate policy recommended for those above 65.

  • 🏢

    Group PA Policy

    Businesses can cover all employees under a single Group PA policy — significant premium discount for groups of 10+.

💡 Group Policy for Business Owners? If you employ drivers, delivery staff, field workers, or labourers — a Group PA policy is mandatory under Workmen's Compensation Act and costs very little per employee. Agent handles group policy setup for any business size.

Coverage & Protection Options

What does Personal Accident Insurance cover?

Comprehensive coverage across all types of accidents, injuries, and disabilities — from minor fractures to accidental death.

💀

Death & Total Disability

  • Accidental Death — 100% sum insured to nominee
  • Permanent Total Disability — 100% sum insured
  • Loss of Both Eyes — 100% sum insured
  • Loss of Both Limbs (hands/feet) — 100% SI
  • Loss of One Eye + One Limb — 100% SI
  • Total & Irrecoverable Loss of Sight / Hearing
🦾

Partial Disability

  • Loss of One Hand / Foot — 50% sum insured
  • Loss of One Eye — 50% sum insured
  • Loss of Big Toe — 20% sum insured
  • Loss of Any Finger — 10%–15% sum insured
  • Loss of Hearing (one ear) — 25–30% SI
  • Loss of Speech (permanent) — 50% sum insured
🏥

Hospitalisation & Medical

  • Accident hospitalisation expenses reimbursed
  • Ambulance charges covered
  • ICU / surgery charges for accident injuries
  • Follow-up treatment expenses
  • Physiotherapy & rehabilitation costs (some plans)
  • Medical equipment rental (wheelchair, crutches)
🦴

Fracture & Burn Benefits

  • Fracture of Femur / Hip Bone — up to ₹50,000
  • Fracture of Leg / Arm — up to ₹25,000
  • Fracture of Wrist / Ankle — up to ₹10,000
  • Third Degree Burns (>20% body) — fixed benefit
  • Second Degree Burns (>10% body) — fixed benefit
  • Dislocation of Major Joint — fixed benefit payout
💼

Add-On Coverage Options

  • Education Grant for children (on parent's death)
  • Loan EMI protection (on total disability)
  • Daily hospital cash — ₹500–₹2,000/day
  • Adventure sports cover (trekking, bungee)
  • Terrorism / riot accident cover
  • Funeral / last rites expenses (fixed benefit)
🌍

Coverage Scope & Exclusions

  • Worldwide 24/7 coverage — any location
  • Road, rail, air accidents all covered
  • Workplace accidents covered
  • Excluded: self-inflicted injury / suicide
  • Excluded: war / nuclear hazard
  • Excluded: accident under influence of alcohol

Plan Benefits

Why Personal Accident Insurance is essential

Accidents don't announce themselves. But their financial impact — hospital bills, income loss, family burden — can be planned for.

💀 Family's Financial Security on Death

Accidental death is the most financially devastating event for a family. Full sum insured paid to nominee immediately — covers outstanding loans, children's education, and family livelihood.

♿ Income Replacement on Disability

Permanent disability means no income — but EMIs, rent, and expenses continue. PA insurance pays up to 100% sum insured on total disability — replacing lost income.

💰 No Medical Tests — Instant Cover

Unlike health or life insurance, personal accident policies require no medical examination. Any age, any health condition — instant issue. Premium based on occupation and age only.

🌍 24/7 Worldwide Coverage

Whether at work, on the road, traveling abroad, or at home — PA cover is active 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, anywhere in the world. No geographic restrictions.

📈 Extremely Affordable Premium

₹25 Lakhs personal accident cover costs just ₹150–₹300/month for a desk job professional. For field workers or drivers, still under ₹600/month. Lowest cost insurance available.

💼 Complements Health & Life Insurance

Health insurance covers medical bills. Life insurance covers natural death. PA insurance covers the gap — accident-specific death, disability, and fracture benefits that others don't pay.
