Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Working Capital Loan?

Manufacturers, traders, retailers, and service providers can all qualify with stable business operations.

🏢 Business Eligibility Basics

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 2–3 years of operations preferred

  • 💰

    Annual Turnover

    Minimum ₹10 Lakhs annual turnover for most lenders

  • 📊

    CIBIL / Credit Score

    680 or above preferred for promoter and business

  • 🧾

    GST & ITR Filing

    Regular GST returns & ITR filing strengthens application

💡 Seasonal businesses (agriculture-linked traders, festival-based retailers) get specially structured cash credit limits — agent will match the right scheme.

📋 Entity Types Eligible

  • 🏪

    Sole Proprietorship

    Individual-owned businesses with valid registration

  • 🤝

    Partnership Firms

    Registered partnership firms with partnership deed

  • 🏢

    Private Limited Companies

    Companies with audited financials and board resolution

  • 🌾

    LLPs & MSME-Registered Units

    Udyam-registered MSMEs get preferential schemes

💡 Udyam (MSME) registration significantly improves eligibility and unlocks government-backed schemes like CGTMSE. Agent will guide if you don't have one yet.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Working capital loans require business financials along with standard KYC. We collect digitally or from your office.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card — individual & business (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Registered office address proof

🏢 Business Registration Documents

  • Udyam / MSME registration certificate
  • GST registration certificate
  • Partnership deed / MOA-AOA (if applicable)
  • Shop & establishment licence

💰 Financial Documents

  • Last 12 months bank statement (current account)
  • Last 2–3 years ITR with audited financials
  • GST returns (last 12 months)
  • Existing loan / credit facility statements

📸 Photographs & Others

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs (promoters)
  • Signed application form
  • Stock & debtor statement (for CC limit)
  • Collateral documents (if opting for secured loan)

📱 Initial documents can be shared digitally via WhatsApp — agent visits your office for verification.

Loan Amount, Tenure & Rates

What can you expect on your Working Capital Loan?

Indicative figures based on business profile and facility type — agent gives you exact figures after eligibility check.

🏦 Cash Credit / Overdraft

  • Limit based on stock & receivables
  • Rate: 10.5% – 14% p.a. (on utilised amount)
  • Tenure: 12 months, renewable annually
  • Amount: ₹5L – ₹2 Crores

📄 Working Capital Term Loan

  • Fixed amount disbursed upfront
  • Rate: 11% – 15% p.a.
  • Tenure: 12 – 36 months
  • Amount: ₹2L – ₹1.5 Crores

🔓 Unsecured Business Line

  • No collateral required
  • Rate: 13% – 16% p.a.
  • Tenure: 12 – 24 months
  • Amount: ₹2L – ₹50 Lakhs

📊 Processing & Charges

  • Processing fee: 0.5% – 2% of sanctioned limit
  • Renewal charges apply annually
  • CGTMSE guarantee fee (if applicable)
  • Documentation & stamp duty charges
