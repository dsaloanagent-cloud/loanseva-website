Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply for a Commercial Vehicle Loan?

Transporters, fleet owners, and first-time vehicle buyers can all qualify with the right documentation.

🚛 Individual / First-Time Buyers

  • 🎂

    Age

    21 to 65 years at loan maturity

  • 🪪

    Driving / Commercial Licence

    Valid commercial driving licence preferred (own or driver employed)

  • 💰

    Income Proof

    Bank statements / ITR showing repayment capacity

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    650 or above preferred; first-timers assessed case-by-case

💡 First-time buyers with no credit history can still apply — agent matches you to lenders with relaxed first-time buyer schemes.

🏢 Fleet Owners / Business Entities

  • 📅

    Business Vintage

    Minimum 2 years of transport / business operations

  • 🚚

    Existing Fleet

    Existing vehicles in good repayment standing strengthen application

  • 💰

    Turnover / Income

    ITR & bank statements showing stable cash flow

  • 📊

    CIBIL Score

    680 or above preferred for best rates and fastest approval

💡 Multi-vehicle / fleet loans can be structured together for better combined interest rates — agent will guide on the best approach.

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready

Commercial vehicle loans require both KYC and vehicle-related paperwork. We collect digitally or from your home/office.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving Licence
  • Recent utility bill for address proof

💰 Income & Financial Documents

  • Last 6 months bank statement
  • Last 2–3 years ITR with financials
  • GST returns (for business/fleet owners)
  • Existing loan / EMI statements (if any)

🚛 Vehicle-Related Documents

  • Quotation / proforma invoice (new vehicle)
  • RC book & insurance copy (used vehicle)
  • Vehicle valuation report (used vehicle)
  • Permit / fitness certificate (if applicable)

📸 Photographs & Others

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Signed application form
  • Co-applicant / guarantor documents (if applicable)
  • Business registration proof (fleet owners)

📱 All documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp — no branch visit needed. Agent comes to your home or office.
